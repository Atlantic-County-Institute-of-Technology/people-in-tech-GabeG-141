Jack Ma the founder of Alibaba

Jack Ma was born on September 10th 1964 in Hangzhou, Zhejiang province, China. Ma was born into poverty and was interested in the English Language. This led him to work as a guide in his teens for foreign tourists to Hangzhou. After his 3rd attempt he was admitted to Hangzhou Tachers college in 1984 where he earned a bachelors degree in English in 1988. In 1995 Ma took a trip to the US on behalf of the Hangzhou city government
